Introduction

This is the official Revisioned Studios website! We'll keep all of our creations and links here for you to see. We create games, websites, and in the future; so much more! This website will showcase ways to make games, and cool features that you can add to your game! Since we're only in Rec Room so far, we'll be putting up courses to help you with creating games on Rec Room! The courses featured in the shop will contain mostly things catered towards horror games, but will include other fun game features too.


Game Descriptions:

  • Backrooms Revisioned is a game on Rec Room, with multiple mazes and levels to choose from, the unique mechanics featured in this game makes it fun and challenging for most!

  • Liminal Roomz is a spinoff of Backrooms Revisioned, with a more liminal look to it. It goes off of this liminality and is more of a exploring game than a horror game, it's a nice change of pace from Backrooms Revisioned.
